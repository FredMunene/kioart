## SLIDE CONTENT:

**The Problems Artists Face Today**:

1. **No Consistent Marketplace**
   - Artists create beautiful work but have nowhere to sell it regularly
   - Baraza's gallery stores art, but it doesn't move fast enough
   - Social media posts get lost in the noise
   - No centralized, trusted platform exists

2. **Can't Reach Buyers**
   - Local buyers don't know where to find quality Kenyan art
   - International buyers can't discover or access Kenyan artists
   - Diaspora Kenyans want to buy local art but have no easy way
   - Corporate buyers need art but don't know where to source it

3. **Irregular Income**
   - Artists only sell during special events or festivals
   - Income is unpredictable and unsustainable
   - Can't plan or grow their art business
   - Many talented artists give up and pursue other careers

4. **Manual, Inefficient Processes**
   - Artists must physically bring art to galleries
   - Pricing is often guesswork
   - Payment collection is manual and slow
   - No data on what sells or what buyers want

**The Cost of Doing Nothing**:
- 200+ talented Baraza artists not reaching their potential
- Thousands of shillings worth of art sitting unsold
- Artists leaving the creative economy for other work
- Kenya's rich artistic heritage undervalued globally

A comprehensive online marketplace where:

- **Technology** handles payments, delivery, and marketing automatically

1. **Easy Upload System**

   - Fill simple form (title, description, price)
   - Publish in minutes - art goes live immediately
   - No technical skills needed

   - Timed auctions (for special pieces)
   - Commission requests (custom artwork)
   - NFT minting option (digital ownership certificates)

   - M-Pesa integration (most important for Kenya!)
   - Credit/debit cards (international buyers)
   - Cryptocurrency (for NFT collectors)
   - Artists get paid within 24-48 hours

4. **Built-in Marketing**
   - Artist profiles showcase portfolios
   - Search by category, price, location
   - Social media sharing tools
   - Email marketing to buyers
   - "Baraza Verified" badge for trust

5. **Gallery Integration**
   - Physical art can be stored at Baraza
   - Buyers can pickup or choose delivery
   - Baraza earns storage fees
   - Hybrid online/offline model

**Why It Will Work**:
- **Simple**: If you can use WhatsApp, you can use JamiiSanaa
- **Trusted**: Baraza partnership provides credibility
- **Fair**: Artists keep 85-90% (vs. 50% at traditional galleries)

- **Scalable**: Start with Kisumu, expand to all of Kenya
